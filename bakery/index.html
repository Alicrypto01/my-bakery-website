MISSION

More Information

Bakery business has gained immense popularity over the years, owing to the increasing demand for baked products. A bakery offers a wide range of products, from bread to pastries, cookies, and cakes, among others. With the growing customer base and market potentiality of bakery items, it is crucial for the bakery owners to expand their business to cater to the rising demand. This paper explores the expansion of Raisin Sun Bakery and ensure continued growth in the market.


OUR BRIEF HISTORY


The business started as a hobby and passion for confectionery products which Amina Gunda baked at home and sold to close family members and the news spread. Amina a graduate of Diploma in Human Resources Management carried out research of the bakery industry and coupled with her vast experience in hotel and health care industries took a bold step to turn this hobby into an income generating venture. She sold her personal car to raise capital to purchase equipment for a fully-fledged bakery unit. Amina Gunda, is a fairly successful businesswomen especially in the real estate also runs a realtor business and she used income from the real estate business to periodically finance the expansion of the new bakery unit. After getting advise from KEBS on what to do to enable reach the general populous, she registered the business 2 years ago under the name of Raisin Sun Bakery and has been able establish solid track record in providing excellent products and services that meet the expectations of all its clients. The production unit is strategically located in an area that is far from traditional super markets so that she tap into the market in the surrounding household and the small kiosks who have to rely for confectioneries from supermarkets and other suppliers who are far off which is disadvantageous in terms of transport cost, timely delivery and freshness of the products.
